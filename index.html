Welcome to Knownodes!

Knownodes is a free tool for your community to organise their knowledge and actions. It empowers everyone to connect between web resources and insights as well as review, visualise, follow and share these connections.

Knownodes is open-source and simple to customise and adjust to the needs of your community.
